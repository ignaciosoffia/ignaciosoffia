Hey, this is Ignacio

I'm a digital product designer from Chile. I use design as a tool to turn technology into delightful experiences.

Design is everywhere, but good design is not. I'm committed to change that. It is not about the form, nor the content. As Paul Rand once said, it is about the relationship between those elements. Good product design it not just good looks or pure function. The best products engage with feeling and empower users to achieve more. I want people to understand technology and do amazing things with it.

Built my first startup at 17 with the goal of combining tech and design to make education in Chile more accesible. Studied Design at the Pontifical Catholic University of Chile from 2015 to 2020.

Currently in Santiago working at Fintual alongside the most talented team, changing the way people perceive savings, investment and wealth in general.
